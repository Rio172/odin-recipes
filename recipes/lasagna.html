Homemade Italian Lasagna

"Discover the only lasagna recipe you'll ever need! Our meaty, cheesy, crowd-pleasing Simply Lasagna Recipe is perfect for any occasion.

Made with lean ground beef, part-skim ricotta cheese, fresh parsley, and mozzarella cheese, this lasagna is sure to satisfy. Try it tonight and taste the difference!"

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
